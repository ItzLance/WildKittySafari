Explore Our Safari

Explore the wild up close and be able to see in person our wild cats in their natural habitat, take a trip through the safari by car or take a guided bus tour and snap amazing shots of our cats. goals in the creation of Wild Kitty Safari was to have an attraction that was reasonably priced, allowing the proper care of the animals and grounds but also staying affordable to young families, especially those interested in reaching 3- to 12-year-olds to help them gain a lifelong love of animals and nature. Where To Find Us

We are wildlife specialists, not led by commissions but instead by putting people in the right place at the right time. When you get in touch you’ll discover what makes our trips so different. Our award winning safaris can’t be replicated by other companies. A destination specialist will be in touch; we don’t do hard sales but you will get plenty of inspiration and advice, as well as help creating your next trip.
